E-Commerce Sales Dashboard Insights

1. Maharashtra generated the highest sales amount (~102K), followed by Madhya Pradesh (~87K).

2. Clothing contributed the highest quantity sold (63%), making it the top-performing category by volume.

3. Electronics accounted for 21% of quantity sold, while Furniture contributed 17%.

4. COD (Cash on Delivery) was the most preferred payment mode (~44%), indicating customer trust in offline payment methods.

5. UPI became the second most used payment option (~21%), showing growing digital payment adoption.

6. Printers generated the highest profit among sub-categories.

7. Tables showed comparatively lower profitability and may require pricing or cost optimization.

8. Monthly profit trends indicate:
   - Strong profits during January, February, March, and November.
   - Losses observed in May, July, September, and December.

9. Harivansh and Madhav were the highest contributing customers in terms of purchase amount.

10. Seasonal sales spikes suggest strong festive or promotional period influence on revenue generation.
